Weapons/Renegade

Renegade

Battle Rifle•Rare

Overview

The Renegade is a lever-action Battle Rifle that uses Medium Ammo. When fully upgraded, it is a powerful weapon in the hands of a skilled marksman. Although it favors medium- to long-range engagements, it is no slouch when taking the fight inside. Raiders who are crouching and stationary can bullseye targets down range. Equipping the Renegade with a Silencer II or better deafens every shot. Reloading is slow; every shell is loaded individually.
